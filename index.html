The three quiet problems no one warns you about

Dear Heirloom helps you capture what matters while it is still easy - so your family is never left guessing.

1
Stories fade

Photos survive, but the meaning vanishes. The job you almost did not take. The day you met your partner. The lesson you learned the hard way. Capture it while you can still tell it in your own words.

2
Documents are scattered

Your Will, superannuation, insurance policies, Power of Attorney, property documents, bank accounts, passwords - scattered across drawers, emails, and places only you know. That needs to change.

3
Family feels unprepared

When something happens, your loved ones face decisions - often without context, contacts, or a clear plan. The greatest gift you can give them is knowing exactly what to do and where to find everything.

How it works

Three simple steps. No tech skills needed. Start with stories, add documents when you are ready.

1
Start with your stories

Each week, Dear Heirloom asks you one warm, thoughtful question - about your childhood, your career, your family, your values. You answer in your own words. The AI helps turn your answer into a beautiful, readable memory. No writing experience needed.

2
Organise what matters

When you are ready, add your important documents - Will, Power of Attorney, superannuation, insurance, property, medical directives, bank accounts, digital passwords, and more. There is no limit to what you can store. You are in control of what gets added and when.

3
Your family is always ready

You choose who can see what, and when. You can share selected stories now - or set up trusted access so your family automatically receives everything if something happens to you. You stay in control. They are never left in the dark.

What if something happens before I share?

This is the most important question - and Dear Heirloom has a clear answer. You can nominate a trusted person right now who will automatically receive access to everything you have stored. You do not need to make that decision in the moment. You set it once, update it anytime, and know your family will never be left searching.

  • 1
    Nominate a trusted person - a spouse, adult child, or close friend. They do not get access until it is needed.
  • 2
    Set your trigger - you decide what grants them access. You can update this anytime.
  • 3
    They receive everything - stories, documents, contacts, wishes - clearly organised and ready to act on.

A note from Si

My name is Sidhika - most people call me Si. I grew up in Cronulla, Sydney, in a family full of stories. My grandfather could hold a room for hours. My grandmother kept every letter, every photo, every little scrap of paper that mattered.

When my grandfather passed, we spent weeks searching for documents we did not know existed and trying to remember stories we had never thought to write down. We found fragments - but not nearly enough. That loss never quite left me.

Dear Heirloom is what I wish had existed then. Not a filing cabinet. Not a diary app. Something that gently helps the people you love most capture what matters - their voice, their wisdom, their life - and keep everything organised for the family that will one day need it.

If you have a parent, a grandparent, or someone in your life with stories worth keeping - I built this for them. And for you.

- Si, Cronulla, Sydney
