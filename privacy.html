Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2025

The short version: Your stories and documents are yours. We store them securely in Australia. We never sell, share, or access your personal content. Ever.

1. Who we are

Dear Heirloom is an Australian-owned and operated service. Our registered business address is in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. You can contact us at hello@dearheirloom.com.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

2. Information we collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Content you create: Life stories, memories, and any text you write within the platform.
  • Documents you upload: Any files you choose to store in your document vault.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the service, to improve your experience.

3. How we use your information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the Dear Heirloom service. Specifically:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store and display your stories and documents
  • To send you weekly story prompts and service notifications
  • To process your subscription payments
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve the platform based on how it is used

We will never sell your personal information, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose other than providing Dear Heirloom to you.

4. Where your data is stored

All personal data, stories, and uploaded documents are stored on servers located in Australia (Sydney region). Your data does not leave Australia. We use Amazon Web Services (AWS) ap-southeast-2 region for secure file storage.

5. Who can access your content

Only you can access your stories and documents. Family members or trusted persons you explicitly nominate within the platform will only receive access according to the settings you configure. Dear Heirloom staff do not access your personal content except in exceptional circumstances required to provide technical support, and only with your explicit permission.

6. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Our security measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive documents
  • Secure authentication via industry-standard providers
  • Regular security reviews

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in serious harm, we will notify affected individuals and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) as required under the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

7. Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Complain about how we handle your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@dearheirloom.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies only - to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

9. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate Dear Heirloom:

  • Stripe - payment processing (governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Amazon Web Services - file storage in Australia
  • Anthropic Claude API - AI-powered story prompts (no personal content is retained by Anthropic)

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by email or through the platform. Continued use of Dear Heirloom after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at:

Dear Heirloom
Email: hello@dearheirloom.com
Sydney, New South Wales, Australia

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at oaic.gov.au.
